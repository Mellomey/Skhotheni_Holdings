Our Mission

Skhotheni Holdings was born from a deeply personal journey of transformation, resilience, and spiritual awakening. Founded by a woman who once walked the path of a Christian pastor and missionary, her life took a profound turn following a near-death experience. In that moment, it wasn’t doctrine that reached her, but the ancestral presence of her great-grandmother and great-grandfather—both traditional healers—who reawakened her purpose. Leaving behind a life rooted in Christian theology, she embraced her ancestral calling, even as it meant being disowned by her family and facing homelessness. But through divine guidance and ancestral faithfulness, she overcame adversity and rose stronger, not only for herself but for others walking through life’s difficult seasons. Skhotheni Holdings exists for those navigating their own “Skhotheni” moments—times of uncertainty, displacement, or transformation. Whether you're seeking healing, direction, support, or opportunity, our mission is to stand with you. We are a multi-service provider built to meet personal, spiritual, health, and lifestyle needs with compassion, inclusion, and unwavering support. Our slogan, "Built for Every Challenge," reflects who we are—resilient, adaptable, and deeply rooted in the belief that everyone deserves guidance, care, and hope.
